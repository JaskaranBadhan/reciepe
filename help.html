Help

Account management

  • Create an Account: Click on the "Sign Up" button and fill out the required information.
  • Log In: Enter your username and password in the login form and click "Log In".
  • Log Out: Click on your username and select "Log Out" from the dropdown menu.
  • Update Account Information: Go to your account settings page to update your profile, password, or email.

Searching for Recipes

  • Search by Keyword: Enter a keyword related to the recipe you're looking for in the search bar and press "Enter".
  • Search by Category: Browse through different categories such as breakfast, lunch, dinner, dessert, etc.
  • Search by Ingredients: Use the ingredient filter to find recipes that include specific ingredients.
  • Using Filters: Refine your search results by applying filters such as dietary preferences, cooking time, and difficulty level.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

  • Forgot Password: If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.
  • Error Messages: If you encounter any error messages while submitting a recipe or searching for recipes, please check the provided information for any mistakes or contact our support team for assistance.
